About

UBA is the first of its kind. We are a project-based club with the goal of improving the “quality of life” for students in Diablo Valley College.We drive ourselves to finding solutions to common problems that are related to technology, marketing, and consulting.

Industry-standard is the quality of work we strive for. Our executive officers all understand the type of work that will change the community UBA resides in, we settle for nothing less.

Come and join us on our mission to establish change in our community, when no one else in bothers too.

Projects

Phi Beta Lambda

Team Leads: Andrew Pajes, Lyes Makoudi

Phi Beta Lambda one of the Biggest Organization’s on Diablo Valley College’s Campus, has provided excellent leadership and services for many years within DVC’s community. As UBA, we are striving to refine their knowledge on leadership & marketing strategies. Andrew Pajes, The Project Lead & Vice President of Operations of UBA , understands the potential of the leaders in today’s world & the power they have to impact or define a working culture. As UBA, not only do we share valuable insight into each organization we work with, but we also strive to generate effective strategies on how to properly accomplish ones vision/goals.
